# GDPR and CCPA Compliance

SavSpot is designed to help your business meet its obligations under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA).

## How SavSpot Handles Data Privacy

SavSpot acts as a data processor on behalf of your business (the data controller). All client data is stored in isolated, tenant-specific partitions, ensuring that one business's data is never accessible to another.

| Principle | Implementation |
|-----------|----------------|
| **Data minimization** | Only data required for the booking process is collected. Custom fields are optional. |
| **Storage limitation** | You control retention periods for client data in your settings. |
| **Security** | Data is encrypted at rest and in transit. Access is restricted by role-based permissions. |
| **Audit trail** | All data access and modifications are logged for accountability. |

## Data Subject Access Requests (DSAR)

When a client requests a copy of their personal data:

1. Go to **Clients** and search for the individual by name or email.
2. Open the client profile and click **Export Client Data**.
3. SavSpot generates a downloadable file containing all data associated with that client, including bookings, payments, communications, and consent records.
4. Provide the file to the client within the required timeframe (30 days under GDPR).

## Right to Deletion

To honor a deletion request:

1. Go to the client's profile.
2. Click **Delete Client Data**.
3. Confirm the deletion. This permanently removes all personal data associated with the client.

Transactional records required for legal or accounting purposes (such as payment amounts and dates) are anonymized rather than deleted, retaining the business record without personally identifiable information.

## Data Processing Agreement

SavSpot provides a Data Processing Agreement (DPA) for businesses that require one. You can request and sign the DPA from **Settings > Privacy > Data Processing Agreement**.

## Cookie Consent

The SavSpot booking page and embed widget use only essential cookies required for the booking flow to function. No tracking or analytics cookies are set by SavSpot on your booking page.

> **Tip:** If you use the embed widget on a website that sets its own tracking cookies, ensure your site's cookie consent banner covers those cookies. SavSpot's widget does not require separate cookie consent.
