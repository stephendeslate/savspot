# Automated Reminders

Reduce no-shows by sending automated reminders to clients before their appointments. SavSpot can send reminders via email and SMS at configurable intervals so clients always know when their next appointment is coming up.

## Setting Up Reminders

1. Navigate to **Settings > Communications** (`/settings/communications`).
2. Find the **Automated Reminders** section.
3. Enable reminders by toggling the feature on.
4. Configure your reminder schedule and channels.
5. Save your settings.

## Reminder Timing

Choose when reminders are sent relative to the appointment time:

| Timing | Best For |
|---|---|
| 24 hours before | Standard reminder giving clients time to prepare or reschedule |
| 1 hour before | Last-minute reminder for same-day awareness |

You can enable one or both timing options. When both are active, clients receive two separate reminders for each appointment.

> **Tip:** The 24-hour reminder is the most effective for reducing no-shows. The 1-hour reminder is useful for businesses where clients frequently run late or forget same-day appointments.

## Reminder Channels

Configure which channels are used to deliver reminders:

| Channel | Details |
|---|---|
| Email | Reminder sent to the client's registered email address |
| SMS | Text message sent to the client's phone number |

You can enable both channels simultaneously. If a client does not have a phone number on file, SMS reminders are skipped for that client.

> **Tip:** SMS reminders tend to have higher open rates than email. If your clients frequently miss email reminders, consider enabling SMS as an additional channel.

## Reminder Content

Reminders use the booking reminder template configured in your communications settings. The default template includes:

- Client name
- Service name
- Appointment date and time
- Business name and location

You can customize the reminder template at **Settings > Communications** (`/settings/communications`). See [Email and SMS Templates](email-and-sms-templates.md) for details on editing templates and using variables.

## Enabling and Disabling Reminders

To turn reminders on or off:

1. Go to **Settings > Communications** (`/settings/communications`).
2. Toggle the **Automated Reminders** switch.
3. Save your changes.

When reminders are disabled, no automated reminder messages are sent. Clients will still receive booking confirmations and other enabled communications.
