# Responding to Reviews

Reply to client reviews directly from the Reviews page to show clients and visitors that you value feedback.

## How to Reply

1. Navigate to **Reviews** (`/reviews`).
2. Find the review you want to respond to.
3. Click the **Reply** button on the review card.
4. Type your response in the text area.
5. Click **Submit Reply**.

## Reply Visibility

All replies are **public**. The reply dialog states: "Your reply will be visible to the client and other visitors." There is no private reply option.

Your reply appears directly below the client's review in an indented block, along with the date you replied.

## Response Best Practices

1. **Respond promptly.** Aim to reply within 24–48 hours.
2. **Thank the client.** Acknowledge their time and feedback.
3. **Be specific.** Reference details from their review.
4. **Stay professional.** Maintain a constructive tone, especially with negative reviews.
5. **Keep it concise.** A few sentences are usually enough.

> **Tip:** Responding to negative reviews publicly and professionally often has a greater positive impact on potential clients than the negative review itself. It demonstrates accountability and a commitment to improvement.
