# Responding to Reviews

Responding to client reviews shows that you care about feedback and are engaged with your clients. SavSpot lets you reply to reviews with both public and private responses.

## Replying to a Review

1. Navigate to `/reviews` and locate the review you want to respond to.
2. Click on the review to open its detail view.
3. Type your response in the reply field.
4. Choose whether the response should be **public** or **private**.
5. Click **Send Response** to submit your reply.

## Public vs Private Responses

| Response Type | Visible To | Best Used For |
|---------------|-----------|---------------|
| Public | Everyone viewing your booking page | Thanking clients, addressing feedback transparently |
| Private | Only you and the reviewing client | Resolving issues, offering compensation, requesting more details |

## Response Best Practices

Writing thoughtful responses can turn reviews into a marketing asset. Follow these guidelines:

1. **Respond promptly.** Aim to reply within 24 to 48 hours of a review being submitted.
2. **Thank the client.** Always start by acknowledging their time and feedback.
3. **Be specific.** Reference details from their review to show you read it carefully.
4. **Stay professional.** Even with negative reviews, maintain a calm and constructive tone.
5. **Offer solutions.** For negative feedback, explain what you have done or will do to address the concern.
6. **Keep it concise.** A few sentences are usually enough. Lengthy responses can feel defensive.

> **Tip:** Responding to negative reviews publicly and professionally often has a greater positive impact on potential clients than the negative review itself. It demonstrates accountability and a commitment to improvement.

## Response Visibility

Public responses appear directly beneath the client's review on your booking page. Private responses are delivered to the client but are never shown publicly. You can view all of your sent responses, both public and private, from the review detail view on your dashboard.

> **Tip:** If a client updates their review after your response, you will receive a notification so you can follow up if needed.
