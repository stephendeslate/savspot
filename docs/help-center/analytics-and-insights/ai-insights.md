# AI Insights

SavSpot uses AI to analyze your business data and surface actionable recommendations. Navigate to **Insights** (`/insights`) to view your personalized insights.

## Insight Tabs

The page is organized into four tabs, each focusing on a different aspect of your business. Each tab is independently subscription-gated — an upgrade banner is shown if your plan does not include that insight type.

### Demand Insights

AI-generated observations about booking demand patterns. Each insight card shows:

- **Priority** badge (HIGH, MEDIUM, or LOW)
- **Type** badge
- **Title** and description
- **Date** generated
- **Dismiss** button (X) to remove an insight you've reviewed

### Benchmarks

Compare your business metrics against industry averages:

- **Metric name** with your value vs. industry average
- **Percentile** badge: green (75th+), yellow (50th+), red (below 50th)
- **Progress bar** showing your position

### Upsell Recommendations

AI-suggested upsell opportunities:

| Column | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Client** | Client name |
| **Recommended Service** | Suggested service to offer |
| **Confidence** | Confidence score as a percentage badge |
| **Reason** | Why this recommendation was made |

### At-Risk Clients

Clients who may be at risk of not returning:

| Column | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Client** | Client name |
| **Risk Score** | Risk level badge (High/Medium/Low with percentage) |
| **Last Visit** | Date of most recent booking |
| **Total Bookings** | Lifetime booking count |
| **Suggested Action** | Recommended re-engagement action |

> **Tip:** Insights improve over time as more data is collected. Businesses with at least three months of booking history receive the most accurate recommendations.
