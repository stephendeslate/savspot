# AI Insights

SavSpot uses AI to analyze your business data and surface actionable recommendations. Instead of digging through reports yourself, AI Insights highlights opportunities and potential issues automatically.

## Accessing AI Insights

Navigate to `/insights` to view your personalized recommendations. Insights are updated regularly based on your latest booking, revenue, and client data.

## Types of Recommendations

AI Insights covers three key areas of your business:

### Scheduling Optimization

| Insight | Example |
|---------|---------|
| Underutilized time slots | "Tuesdays between 2 PM and 5 PM have 80% availability. Consider offering a promotion for these slots." |
| Peak demand windows | "Saturday mornings consistently book out 2 weeks in advance. Consider extending morning availability." |
| Buffer time suggestions | "Your 60-minute services average 68 minutes. Adding a 15-minute buffer could reduce back-to-back delays." |

### Pricing Suggestions

| Insight | Example |
|---------|---------|
| Underpriced services | "Your Deep Tissue Massage has a 95% booking rate, well above average. A price increase may be sustainable." |
| Discount effectiveness | "Your 10% new client discount converts 3x better than no discount, but 15% and 20% show no additional lift." |
| Revenue per hour analysis | "Your Express Facial generates $120/hour vs $85/hour for your Standard Facial. Consider promoting it more." |

### Client Retention

| Insight | Example |
|---------|---------|
| At-risk clients | "12 clients who booked monthly have not returned in 6+ weeks. Sending a check-in message could re-engage them." |
| Rebooking patterns | "Clients who rebook within 24 hours of their appointment have a 90% retention rate over 6 months." |
| Service bundling | "Clients who book Service A are 4x more likely to also book Service B. Consider offering them as a package." |

## How Insights Are Generated

AI Insights are generated by analyzing patterns across your business data, including:

1. **Booking history** — Frequency, timing, service mix, and cancellation patterns.
2. **Revenue trends** — Growth rates, seasonal variation, and per-service profitability.
3. **Client behavior** — Return rates, booking preferences, and engagement signals.

Insights are specific to your business. They reflect your actual data, not industry averages.

> **Tip:** Check your insights weekly. Acting on even one recommendation per week can lead to meaningful improvements in revenue and client satisfaction over a quarter.

> **Tip:** Insights improve over time as more data is collected. Businesses with at least three months of booking history receive the most accurate and actionable recommendations.
