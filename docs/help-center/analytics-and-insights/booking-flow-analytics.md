# Booking Flow Analytics

Booking flow analytics help you understand how clients move through your booking process, where they drop off, and what you can do to convert more visitors into confirmed bookings.

## Tracking the Client Booking Funnel

The booking funnel visualizes each step a client takes from landing on your booking page to completing a booking. SavSpot tracks the following stages:

| Stage | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| Page View | Client visits your booking page |
| Service Selection | Client selects a service |
| Time Selection | Client picks an available date and time |
| Details Entry | Client fills in their personal information |
| Confirmation | Client confirms and completes the booking |

Each stage shows the number of clients who reached it and the percentage who continued to the next step.

## Identifying Drop-off Points

The funnel chart highlights where clients leave the booking process. Common patterns include:

- **High drop-off at service selection** — Clients may not find a service that matches their needs, or pricing may not be visible enough.
- **High drop-off at time selection** — Limited availability or confusing calendar display could be the cause.
- **High drop-off at details entry** — The form may be asking for too much information.
- **High drop-off at confirmation** — Payment friction or unexpected costs at checkout.

> **Tip:** Focus your optimization efforts on the stage with the highest drop-off rate. A small improvement at the biggest bottleneck will have the largest impact on overall conversions.

## Conversion Rates

The dashboard displays two key conversion metrics:

1. **Overall conversion rate** — The percentage of page visitors who complete a booking.
2. **Step-by-step conversion rate** — The percentage of clients who advance from each stage to the next.

Track these metrics over time to measure the impact of changes you make to your booking flow.

## Optimizing Based on Data

Use your booking flow data to make targeted improvements:

1. **Review drop-off points** weekly to spot new issues.
2. **Simplify underperforming steps** by reducing required fields or adding clearer instructions.
3. **Test changes** by comparing conversion rates before and after modifications.
4. **Check device breakdown** to ensure the booking flow works well on both desktop and mobile.

> **Tip:** Even a 5% improvement in conversion rate can translate to meaningful revenue growth over time. Small, data-driven changes compound.
