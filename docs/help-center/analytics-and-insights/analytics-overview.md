# Analytics Overview

The analytics dashboard gives you a clear picture of how your business is performing. Track revenue, bookings, client growth, and service popularity from a single page.

## Accessing the Dashboard

Navigate to `/analytics` to open your analytics dashboard. The dashboard displays key metrics at the top with detailed reports below.

## Available Reports

SavSpot provides the following built-in reports:

| Report | What It Shows |
|--------|---------------|
| Revenue | Total revenue, revenue by service, and revenue trends over time |
| Bookings | Booking volume, completion rates, and cancellation rates |
| Clients | New client acquisition, returning client rates, and client demographics |
| Services | Most popular services, average booking value per service, and utilization rates |

## Key Metrics

The top of the dashboard displays your most important numbers at a glance:

- **Total Revenue** — Sum of all completed booking payments in the selected period.
- **Total Bookings** — Number of bookings created in the selected period.
- **New Clients** — Number of first-time clients acquired.
- **Average Booking Value** — Mean revenue per completed booking.
- **Cancellation Rate** — Percentage of bookings that were cancelled.

## Date Range Filters

Use the date range selector to focus on the period that matters to you:

1. Click the date range picker at the top of the dashboard.
2. Select a preset range (today, this week, this month, this quarter, this year) or choose custom dates.
3. The dashboard updates automatically to reflect the selected period.

> **Tip:** Compare the same period across different years to identify seasonal patterns. If bookings dip at a certain time every year, you can plan promotions in advance.

## Exporting Data

To export your analytics data:

1. Navigate to the report you want to export.
2. Click the **Export** button in the top right corner.
3. Choose your format: CSV for spreadsheet analysis or PDF for sharing.
4. The file downloads to your device immediately.

Exported data includes all records within your selected date range and any active filters.

> **Tip:** Schedule monthly exports to maintain offline records and share performance summaries with your team or accountant.
