# Public Booking Page

Every SavSpot business gets a dedicated public booking page that clients can use to browse services, check availability, and book appointments — no account required.

## Your Booking URL

Your public booking page is available at:

```
https://app.savspot.com/book/<your-business-slug>
```

You can find your exact URL in **Settings > Booking Page**. The slug is based on your business name and can be customized from the same settings screen.

## What Clients See

When a client visits your booking page, they are guided through a streamlined booking flow:

| Step | Description |
|------|-------------|
| **Service selection** | A list of your active services with names, descriptions, durations, and prices. |
| **Date and time** | An availability calendar showing only open slots based on your schedule and existing bookings. |
| **Client details** | A form to collect the client's name, email, phone, and any custom intake fields you have configured. |
| **Confirmation** | A summary of the booking with payment (if required) and a confirmation message. |

The booking page automatically adapts to your branding settings, displaying your logo, colors, and welcome message.

## Sharing Your Link

There are several ways to share your booking page with clients:

1. **Direct link** — Copy the URL from your dashboard and paste it into emails, social media bios, or text messages.
2. **Website button** — Add a "Book Now" button on your website that links to your booking page.
3. **Email signature** — Include the link in your email signature so every message is an opportunity to book.

## QR Codes

SavSpot generates a QR code for your booking page automatically. To access it:

1. Go to **Settings > Booking Page**.
2. Click **Download QR Code**.
3. Use the downloaded image on printed materials such as business cards, flyers, or in-store signage.

When scanned, the QR code opens your booking page directly in the client's mobile browser.

> **Tip:** Test your booking page from a client's perspective by opening the URL in an incognito browser window. This helps you verify that the flow, branding, and available services look correct before sharing publicly.
