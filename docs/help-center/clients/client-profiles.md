# Client Profiles

Each client in SavSpot has a profile page that brings together their contact information, booking history, payment records, and communication log in one place.

## Accessing a Client Profile

1. Navigate to **Clients** in the sidebar, or go directly to `/clients`.
2. Click on a client's name in the directory list.

The profile page opens with the client's details organized into sections.

## Profile Sections

### Contact Information

The top section displays the client's core details:

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Name** | Full name (first and last). |
| **Email** | Primary email address used for booking confirmations and reminders. |
| **Phone** | Phone number, if provided. |
| **Date Added** | When the client was first created in SavSpot. |

Click **Edit** to update any contact field. Changes are saved immediately and apply to future communications.

### Booking History

A chronological list of all bookings associated with this client. Each entry shows:

- Service name
- Date and time
- Assigned staff member
- Booking status (confirmed, completed, cancelled, no-show)

Click any booking to open its detail view, where you can reschedule, cancel, or update the status.

### Payment History

A record of all payments associated with the client's bookings. Each entry includes the amount, date, payment method, and status (paid, refunded, pending). This section is only visible if your account has payment processing enabled.

### Communication Log

A timeline of emails and notifications sent to the client, including booking confirmations, reminders, and cancellation notices. This helps you verify that the client received important messages.

> **Tip:** Check the communication log if a client says they did not receive a confirmation. You can verify whether the email was sent and resend it if needed.

## Editing a Client Profile

Click **Edit** at the top of the profile to update contact information. You cannot edit booking or payment history directly from the profile -- those records are managed through their respective detail pages.

> **Tip:** If you notice duplicate profiles for the same client, reach out to support to merge them. Duplicate profiles can occur when a client uses different email addresses for separate bookings.
