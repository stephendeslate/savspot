# Client Notes

Client notes let you record important details about individual clients directly on their profile. Notes are visible only to your staff and are never shown to clients.

## When to Use Notes

Notes are useful for tracking information that does not fit into standard profile fields. Common examples include:

- **Preferences** -- "Prefers early morning appointments" or "Likes the front chair"
- **Allergies or sensitivities** -- "Allergic to latex" or "Sensitive to strong fragrances"
- **Special requirements** -- "Requires wheelchair-accessible room" or "Bring interpreter"
- **Past interactions** -- "Mentioned interest in the premium package" or "Had issue with scheduling on Jan 15"

## Adding a Note

1. Navigate to the client's profile at **Clients** > click the client's name.
2. Scroll to the **Notes** section.
3. Click **Add Note**.
4. Type your note in the text field.
5. Click **Save**.

The note is timestamped and associated with the staff member who created it.

## Managing Notes

| Action | How |
|--------|-----|
| **View all notes** | Scroll to the Notes section on the client's profile. Notes are displayed in reverse chronological order (newest first). |
| **Edit a note** | Click the edit icon next to the note you want to change. Update the text and save. |
| **Delete a note** | Click the delete icon next to the note. Confirm the deletion. This action cannot be undone. |

## Note Visibility

Notes are strictly internal. They are visible to all staff members in your business who have access to the client directory. Clients cannot see notes on their profile or in any booking communications.

> **Tip:** Use notes to flag important details before an appointment. For example, if a client mentioned a specific concern during their last visit, add a note so the assigned staff member can review it beforehand.

> **Tip:** Keep notes professional and factual. Since all team members can read them, avoid personal opinions or unnecessary commentary.
