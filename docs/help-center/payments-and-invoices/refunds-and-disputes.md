# Refunds and Disputes

SavSpot does not currently have an in-app refund interface. All refunds and dispute management are handled through your **Stripe Dashboard**.

## Issuing Refunds

To refund a payment:

1. Navigate to **Settings > Payments** (`/settings/payments`).
2. Click **Open Stripe Dashboard**.
3. In the Stripe Dashboard, find the payment under **Payments**.
4. Click the payment, then click **Refund**.
5. Enter the refund amount (full or partial) and submit.

Refunds typically appear on the client's statement within 5–10 business days, depending on their bank.

## Handling Disputes

When a client disputes a charge with their bank, Stripe notifies you through the Stripe Dashboard:

1. Open your **Stripe Dashboard** from Settings > Payments.
2. Navigate to **Disputes** in the Stripe sidebar.
3. Review the dispute details and submit evidence if applicable.

Stripe provides guidance on responding to disputes and the required evidence for each dispute type.

## Cancellation Policies

SavSpot supports cancellation policies that are displayed to clients when they cancel a booking through the portal:

| Policy | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| **Free cancellation** | No charge — full refund applicable |
| **Late cancellation fee** | A fee amount is retained |
| **No refund** | Full amount retained |

These policies are informational — actual refund processing must be done through Stripe.

> **Tip:** Clear cancellation policies reduce disputes. Make sure your policies are configured so clients understand the terms before cancelling.
