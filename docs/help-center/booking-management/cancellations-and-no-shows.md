# Cancellations and No-Shows

Cancellations and no-shows are an inevitable part of running a service business. SavSpot provides tools to handle both situations and track their impact on your business.

## Cancelling a Booking

You can cancel a booking from the booking detail view:

1. Navigate to **Bookings** at `/bookings`
2. Click on the booking you want to cancel
3. Click **Cancel Booking**
4. Confirm the cancellation

Bookings can be cancelled from either the PENDING or CONFIRMED status. Once cancelled, the time slot becomes available for other bookings.

> **Tip:** If the client requests to reschedule rather than cancel, consider changing the date and time on the existing booking instead. This preserves the booking history and avoids gaps in your records.

## Marking a No-Show

When a client does not arrive for their confirmed appointment:

1. Open the booking from the Bookings page or Calendar
2. Click **Mark as No-Show**
3. The status changes to NO_SHOW

No-shows can only be applied to CONFIRMED bookings. A booking in PENDING status should be cancelled rather than marked as a no-show.

## Cancellation Impact

| Area | Effect |
|------|--------|
| **Calendar** | The time slot is freed for new bookings |
| **Revenue** | Cancelled bookings are excluded from revenue calculations |
| **Completion Rate** | Both cancellations and no-shows lower your completion rate |
| **Client History** | The cancellation or no-show is recorded on the client's profile |

## Client No-Show History

SavSpot tracks no-shows per client. You can view a client's no-show count on their profile at `/clients`. This history helps you identify patterns and decide whether to require prepayment or deposits for clients with repeated no-shows.

## Reducing No-Shows

Consider these strategies to improve your completion rate:

- Enable automated booking reminders via email or SMS
- Set up confirmation requests for upcoming appointments
- Require prepayment for services with high no-show rates
- Send a reminder 24 hours before the appointment

> **Tip:** Check your completion rate on the Dashboard regularly. If it drops below 85%, review your reminder and cancellation policy settings.
