# Walk-In Bookings

Walk-in bookings let you record appointments for clients who arrive without a prior reservation. This is common in salons, spas, and other businesses where clients may drop by without scheduling ahead.

## Creating a Walk-In Booking

### From the Dashboard

1. Navigate to `/dashboard`
2. Click the **Walk-In Booking** quick action
3. Select the service being provided
4. Search for the client or create a new client profile
5. Confirm the booking

### From the Bookings Page

1. Navigate to `/bookings`
2. Click **New Booking**
3. Select the service and client
4. Choose the current date and time (or the nearest available slot)
5. Confirm the booking

> **Tip:** The Dashboard quick action is faster for walk-ins because it automatically sets the date and time to now, skipping the scheduling step.

## How Walk-Ins Differ from Standard Bookings

| Aspect | Standard Booking | Walk-In Booking |
|--------|-----------------|-----------------|
| **Timing** | Scheduled in advance | Created for the current time |
| **Initial Status** | PENDING or CONFIRMED | CONFIRMED immediately |
| **Client notification** | Confirmation email sent | No advance notification needed |
| **Calendar** | Shows as a future event | Appears on today's schedule |

## Managing Walk-In Capacity

Walk-in bookings use the same availability system as scheduled bookings. If all staff members are occupied, the system will indicate no available slots. You can still create the booking if you know capacity is available -- the system checks against configured availability, not physical presence.

> **Tip:** If your business relies heavily on walk-ins, consider keeping buffer slots open in your schedule rather than fully booking every time slot.

## Walk-In Clients

When a walk-in client is new, you can create their profile during the booking flow with minimal information -- just a name is sufficient to proceed. You can fill in additional details like email and phone number after the appointment.
