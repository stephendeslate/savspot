# Platform Overview

SavSpot is a multi-tenant SaaS booking platform built for service businesses. Whether you run a salon, event venue, fitness studio, consulting practice, or any other service-based operation, SavSpot provides the tools to manage bookings, clients, payments, and your team from a single dashboard.

## Navigation

The SavSpot sidebar organizes your workspace into four sections:

| Section | Pages |
|---------|-------|
| **Core** | Dashboard (`/dashboard`), Bookings (`/bookings`), Calendar (`/calendar`), Services (`/services`) |
| **Business** | Payments (`/payments`), Clients (`/clients`), Invoices (`/invoices`), Analytics (`/analytics`) |
| **Communication** | Messages (`/messages`), Reviews (`/reviews`), Quotes (`/quotes`), Contracts (`/contracts`) |
| **System** | Imports (`/imports`), Insights (`/insights`), Settings (`/settings`), Help Center (`/help`) |

The sidebar can be collapsed to icon-only mode using the toggle button at the top. On mobile devices, a hamburger menu provides the same navigation.

## How It Works

1. **Create your account** with email or sign in with Google or Apple
2. **Complete the onboarding wizard** to set up your business type and profile
3. **Define your services** with pricing, durations, and availability rules
4. **Share your booking page** with clients or create walk-in bookings manually
5. **Manage your schedule** from the calendar and bookings views
6. **Track performance** through dashboard KPIs and analytics

> **Tip:** During onboarding, selecting your business type pre-configures sensible defaults (services, availability rules, and workflows) so you can get started faster.

## Multi-Tenant Architecture

Each SavSpot account operates as an independent tenant with fully isolated data. Your client information, bookings, and business settings are never shared with or visible to other accounts on the platform. Team members you invite share access to your tenant only.
