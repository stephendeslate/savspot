# Quick Actions

Quick actions on the Dashboard let you jump to common tasks with a single click. They appear in a grid below the stat cards and recommended next steps.

## Available Quick Actions

| Action | Description | Destination |
|--------|-------------|-------------|
| **Add Service** | Create a new bookable service for your clients | `/services/new` |
| **Manage Availability** | Update your working hours and scheduling rules | `/settings/availability` |
| **View Calendar** | Open your calendar to see your upcoming schedule | `/calendar` |

## Using Quick Actions

Each quick action is a card with an icon, name, and brief description. Click any card to navigate directly to the destination page.

These three actions represent the most common tasks for managing a service business day-to-day:

- **Add Service** is useful when you want to expand your offerings or create a new service variant
- **Manage Availability** takes you to your availability rules where you can adjust which days and hours you accept bookings
- **View Calendar** gives you a visual overview of your schedule in day, week, month, or agenda format

> **Tip:** To create a walk-in booking, navigate to **Bookings** (`/bookings`) and click the **Walk-In** button. Walk-in bookings are not available directly from the dashboard quick actions.
